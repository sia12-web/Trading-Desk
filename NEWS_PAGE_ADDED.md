# 📰 Market News Page - ADDED TO DASHBOARD

## ✅ What I Built

A dedicated **Market News** page where you can see all economic events, news headlines, and sentiment analysis - so you can make your own trading decisions!

---

## 🎯 Features

### 1. **Economic Calendar** (Forex Factory)
- ✅ All upcoming economic events (NFP, FOMC, CPI, GDP, etc.)
- ✅ Filter by impact level (HIGH, MEDIUM, LOW)
- ✅ Filter by time range (6h, 24h, 48h, 7 days)
- ✅ Shows time until event (e.g., "in 2h 30m")
- ✅ Forecast vs Previous vs Actual values
- ✅ **IMMINENT** warning for high-impact events <2h away

### 2. **News Sentiment Analysis**
- ✅ AI-analyzed market sentiment (BULLISH/BEARISH/NEUTRAL)
- ✅ Confidence score (0-100%)
- ✅ Currency-specific bias (USD, EUR, GBP, etc.)
- ✅ Key market themes
- ✅ Summary of recent market narrative

### 3. **Recent Headlines**
- ✅ Latest forex news (last 24 hours)
- ✅ Clickable links to full articles
- ✅ Source and timestamp for each headline
- ✅ AI-analyzed for sentiment

### 4. **Pair-Specific Events**
- ✅ Shows events affecting your selected pair
- ✅ Trading recommendations for each event
- ✅ Impact assessment

## 🔔 Key Features You Can Use

### **1. Filter by Impact Level**
See only HIGH impact events (NFP, FOMC, etc.) to avoid trading during major news.

### **2. Select Your Pair**
Choose EUR/USD, GBP/USD, etc. to see events affecting that specific pair.

### **3. Adjust Time Range**
- Next 6 hours → See imminent events
- Next 24 hours → Plan today's trades
- Next 7 days → Weekly outlook

### **4. Check Sentiment Before Trading**
See if news sentiment aligns with your technical analysis:
- ✅ Both bullish → Strong setup
- ⚠️ News bearish, technicals bullish → Conflict, be cautious

### **5. Click Headlines for Details**
Each headline has a link to the full article - click to read more.

## 💡 Pro Tips

### **Before Every Trading Session:**
1. Check Market News page
2. Look for HIGH impact events in next 6 hours
3. If any → avoid trading or reduce size
4. Check sentiment → does it align with your setup?

### **During the Day:**
- Keep Market News page open in another tab
- Refresh every hour to see latest headlines
- Watch for breaking news that might affect your trades

### **Planning Ahead:**
- Set time range to "Next 7 days"
- Mark major events on your calendar
- Plan your trading schedule around NFP, FOMC, CPI

## 🎨 Color Coding

### **Impact Levels:**
- 🔴 **RED** = High Impact (NFP, FOMC, GDP) - AVOID TRADING
- 🟡 **AMBER** = Medium Impact - Be cautious
- 🔵 **BLUE** = Low Impact - Safe to trade

### **Sentiment:**
- 🟢 **GREEN** = BULLISH
- 🔴 **RED** = BEARISH
- 🟡 **AMBER** = MIXED
- ⚪ **NEUTRAL** = No clear bias

### **Warnings:**
- ⚠️ **IMMINENT** = High-impact event <2 hours away
